What's Included

Whether you are a freelancer or an agency, Bashö offers you all the tools you need to present your work in a simple, professional, and coherent manner.

Multiple Layouts

Bashö comes with a wide range of page layouts such as homepage layouts, portfolios, project pages, utility pages and so much more. Choose the ones you need, edit and upload.

20+ Components

Make any design stand out by using a combination of the 20+ components that come with Bashö. Accordions, tabs, buttons, progress bars, maps, pricing tables, counters and a whole lot more.

Premium Plugins

As with all our templates, Bashö comes with 8 premium in-house developed plugins such as sliders, parallax sections, fullscreen sections, and counters. When there is an update you'll be sure to get it.

Purpose-built blocks

Bashö, like all our templates, is built using a block based, responsive framework that makes designing easier. With 40+ blocks at your disposal, not only will you save time, but you can create new layouts with ease.

Awesome Forms

Build simple or complex forms on the fly. Add new fields, select boxes, checkboxes and/or radio buttons to any contact form without editing JS or PHP. Our subscribe forms seamlessly integrate with MailChimp.

Isotope

Bashö includes the popular isotope grid and sorting library which allows you to create amazing masonry grids on the fly. Choose between large, small or no margin grids, animation duration, sorting duration and more.
